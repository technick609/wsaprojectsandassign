Chorasi Town

Chorasi (ususally known as Choryasi) located in Surat is also one of the 182 Legislative Assembly constituencies of Gujarat state in India. Surat city is the part of it.

Chorasi is a town and Taluka in the Surat district of the Gujarat. In India, a Taluka is a subdivision of a district responsible for the administration and revenue collection of a specific area within the district. Talukas play a crucial role in the local governance structure, contributing significantly to the development and administration of their communities.
